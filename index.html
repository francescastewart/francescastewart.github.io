About Us

My name is Francesca Stewart, and I am the creator of Frandles! I am a third-year Software Engineering student at UNBF. My goal with Frandles is to make you your dream candle! Every candle you purchase is hand-poured by myself in my kitchen and made from 100% soy wax.

Getting a Frandle

There are currently three options to get a frandle:
1. Contact me through Instagram DM @frandles.co
2. Contact me through email frandles.company@gmail.com
3. Visit our amazing carriers in person! The beautiful Fresh Vintage NB on xx Regent St Fredericton, or the tasty Calico Cafe on xx in Miramichi!
