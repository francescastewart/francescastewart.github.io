About Us

My name is Francesca Stewart, and I am the creator of Frandles! I am a third-year Software Engineering student at UNBF. My goal with Frandles is to make you your dream candle! Every candle you purchase is hand-poured by myself in my kitchen and made from 100% soy wax.
